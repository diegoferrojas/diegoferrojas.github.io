    Main Description
    The designer role defines the responsibilities, operations, attributes, and relationships of one or several classes, and determines how they will be adjusted to the implementation environment. In addition, the designer role may have responsibility for one or more design packages, or design subsystems, including any classes owned by the packages or subsystems.
